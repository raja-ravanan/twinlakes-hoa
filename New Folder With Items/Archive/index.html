Our neighborhood

A peaceful place to call home

Twin Lakes is a welcoming community in Louisville, Kentucky — surrounded by lush greenery and two serene ponds that bring nature to your doorstep.

  • Beautifully maintained common areas and landscaping
  • Responsive board and professional property management
  • Clear community guidelines that protect property values
  • A welcoming neighborhood for all stages of life
What we offer

Here to serve our community

The Twin Lakes HOA board works year-round to keep the neighborhood safe, beautiful, and well-maintained.

Neighborhood upkeep

Regular landscaping, irrigation maintenance, and pond care keep Twin Lakes looking its best year-round.

Community standards

Our CC&Rs and guidelines ensure the beauty and property values of Twin Lakes are preserved for everyone.
