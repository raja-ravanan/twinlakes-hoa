Important Notice

No Overnight Street Parking

No vehicles are to be parked overnight on subdivision roads at any time. This is especially critical during potential snow events to allow for efficient plowing and salting. Vehicles must be parked overnight in garages or driveways. Overnight parking on community roads violates our CC&Rs.

Trash & Recycling

Garbage Can Enclosure Guidelines

Do’s
  • Obtain ARC approval before construction
  • Place in side or rear yards to minimize visibility
  • Use approved materials: brick, stone, stucco, or painted wood
  • Install a functioning closable door or gate
  • Match or harmonize colors with your home
Don’ts
  • Do not build without ARC approval
  • Do not use unfinished plywood, corrugated metal, or plastic panels
  • Do not use bare soil, mulch, or grass as base
  • Do not leave doors open when not in use
  • Do not ignore violation notices
