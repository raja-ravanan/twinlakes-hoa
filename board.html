Transition Minutes (Oct 2025 – Mar 2026)
Oct 30, 2025 — First Board Working SessionOct 30, 2025

Location: Residence of Mike Schnell. Present: Renee Kuhlman (Mulloy) + Tony Backert, Ramana N, Yashu Basavaraju, Mike Schnell, Aimee Green, Raja Ravanan, Peter Fotos (all 7; quorum met). Peter Fotos later retired in Dec 2025.

First working session of the newly elected homeowner Board. Developer (Elite/Mike Bentley) to step down Dec 31, 2025; Garden-Home construction completing through ~March 2026.

Decisions & Votes
  • Trash vendor → EcoTech (Republic contract expires Dec 31; 60-day notice by Nov 1). Lower cost, 3-year price guarantee (5% cap yrs 4–5), cancellation clause. (Subject to contract execution.)
  • Additional "Residents Only" pond sign — approved 3–2 (2 abstained).
  • Homeowner tree-removal request — denied. Arborist (Lopez) confirmed the tree healthy; Board policy: do not remove healthy trees; consider limb trimming only.
Discussion (deferred)
  • Board terms/officers: Renee recommended 2-year staggered terms; officers to be seated before developer resigns. Deferred.
  • 2026 budget (provisional): must be approved in Nov, mailed by Dec 1. Two associations / one tax ID / separate bank accounts. Garden monthly $175→$185. Home-count to verify. Banking Republic→Western Alliance; payments Zego→ClickPay.
  • Insurance: D&O to be added once developer steps down. Committees recommended (ARC, landscaping, irrigation, grounds, social). Website/portal proposed.
Action Items
  • Renee: EcoTech contract / Republic non-renewal; provide vendor contracts; verify home counts.
  • Board: questions to Mike (bonds, MSD, erosion); finalize budget; schedule internal meeting.
Nov 10, 2025 — Officers & CommitteesNov 10, 2025

Location: Residence of Tony Backert. Board-only session; quorum met. Property manager not present (input relayed).

Decisions
  • Officers elected: President — Tony Backert (6–0); Vice President — Yashu Basavaraju; Treasurer — Ramana N; Secretary — Raja Ravanan; Members at Large — Mike Schnell, Aimee Green, Peter Fotos.
  • Six committees established: ARC, Communication (website/social — Raja w/ Yashu), Contracts/Vendor Relations (incl. insurance & irrigation), Safety (Mike), Social, Beautification & Grounds (Peter Fotos). Finance Committee = board members only, chaired by Treasurer.
  • Process model: committees research → Board decides → President communicates to Renee → Renee executes.
  • EcoTech confirmed with optional yard-waste $5/mo; Renee to confirm collection day.
  • 2026 assessments (provisional): Garden $175→$185/mo; HOA $775→$895/yr to build reserve.
Deferred / Action
  • Questions for developer: reserve left, –$15K (2024), –$24K (2025) deficit, MSD/bonds, ARC codes.
  • Insurance (D&O) review; Facebook + website; first community meeting targeted early March 2026.
Nov 23, 2025 — 2026 Assessment VoteNov 23, 2025

Held via Zoom. Present: Tony, Yashu, Raja, Aimee. Absent: Ramana N (traveling), Peter Fotos.

Decision & Vote

On a motion by Tony Backert, the Board voted in favor to set 2026 assessments: HOA $895/yr (from $775, ~13.5%) and Garden Homes $185/mo (from $175). Impact: ~+$120/yr Patriot, ~+$240/yr Garden. To be locked by Friday, mailed by Dec 1.

Rationale (recorded)
  • First self-funded year; HOA reserve ~$141. Developer obligated to leave only $3,000. Approved dues build ~$9–10K (HOA) + ~$4,500 (Garden) reserve.
  • Cash-flow: annual dues once, garden monthly, costs mid-year — reserve avoids mid-year special assessments.
  • The –$15,000 (2024): Elite put in ~$50K, withdrew ~$15K unspent at year-end.
Action
  • Include explanatory note with dues invoice. Raja: 2025 expenditure review for March. No warranty on irrigation (repair line ~$2,000).
Dec 7, 2025 — Resignation, ARC DecisionsDec 7, 2025

Held via Zoom. Present: Tony, Ramana, Raja, Aimee, Yashu, Mike Schnell (quorum). Absent: Peter Fotos (resigning).

Decisions
  • Resignation of Peter Fotos accepted; Board operates with six through the March meeting, then adds a seventh. Beautification reassigned to Aimee Green.
  • March 10, 2026 community meeting confirmed.
  • Barclay Lake lighting (15805 Barclay Lake Ct): installed without ARC — require an ARC, not approved as-is; must match the approved standard (concealed wiring, matches home; Nov–Jan only).
  • Fence ARC (Ethan): approvable pending maintenance-gap confirmation; adopted a supplemental fence ARC form.
  • EcoTech contract: correct language to the 3-year guarantee.
  • Newsletter: no board-member photos. Minutes: not routinely distributed on request. Cadence: Dec 21 catch-up → Jan 4.
In Progress
  • Insurance: Aimee gathering 5 quotes (renewal March). Ramana: line-by-line 2025 review. Raja: HOA website portal (~$399/yr).

  • Shared HOA Google Drive repository created (by Raja) so records aren't dependent on Renee's inbox.

  • Trash-can screening guidelines to be developed for March (must match home, hide cans, solid floor, ARC required).

  • March 10 confirmed at the church; members prepare 2–3 slides; biweekly meetings at 6:30 PM.
